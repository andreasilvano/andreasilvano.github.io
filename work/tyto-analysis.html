Project

Tyto is an add-on for acoustic wayfinding that can be implemented on every urban navigation app on every platform available. Our job was to analyze social, economic and strategic context/environment; come up with a brilliant and feasible idea, then design everything that had to do with it, until it was ready to be presented to the public/investors.
