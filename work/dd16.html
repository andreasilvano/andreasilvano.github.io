Project

01 - What if e-wallets were planets?
In 2019 e-wallet transactions grew by 884% from 2018 with 37.7% share in overall payments. How many users do they have? How influential can they be in the region they come from and operate in? The visualization focuses on showing the variety of companies present in the market in 2019 as if they were part of a planetary system. Thanks to the “space metaphor” the reader will easily engage in reading and understanding data that is commonly undisclosed or difficult to source.

02 - Instagram-fueled tourism
Have you ever been to a place just because of someone else’s picture on social media? We have, so we decided to better understand why and how this happens. We have been using digital tools to analyze social media platforms (Instagram and TripAdvisor) in order to grasp the variety of tourism categories, to comprehend how a photographic trend spreads across different platforms and to single out specific types of tourism and investigate them thoroughly.

03 - It's Cheaper Abroad
Medical tourism online is advertised with the language of a product of consumption. We transpose real promos of medical tourism found on Instagram into a digital catalogue. The deals and promos on cosmetic surgeries aim at exposing mass marketing language and objectification.
