Wall to wall

+ MORE INFO

Wall to wall meant designing an entire deck of cards, plus a digital platform that you can access online via NFC technology. Every suit is a collection of photographs of a specific district of Milan. The jokers and the aces of each suit are interactive cards with embedded NFC technology: a sticker inside a double sheet of paper contains the information that is deliver to you mobile device through proximity.

Personal work on this project consisted of realizing the entire NFC online platform, embedding the NFC tags into the sheets of paper and of course editing part of the pictures in Photoshop. In fact every number we applied on our photos in post-production had to be tweaked manually in order to achieve the realistic feel of “numbers printed on the wall”. We also designed an A5 comprehensive brochure that explains concept, realization and printing.


by Beatrice Curti and Andrea Silvano
