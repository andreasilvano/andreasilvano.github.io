Work

Compra Fácil - Continente

Self-checkout machines and interfaces are often ignored in terms of design and user experience. This project is an attempt to solve some of the main problems encountered when shopping and checking out with this kind of terminals. Extensive research for over two months was necessary just to understand and replicate the already existing interfaces. Consequently I highlighted the main problems and tried to tackle them from a different perspective. The result is a much faster and cleaner interface. After talking with the actual client on this project I also created a short 1-minute film in order to underline the main features of this new proposition.
