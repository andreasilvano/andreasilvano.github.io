A group project where we designed a magazine from scratch. Each group member was assigned a section of the magazine, all while keeping the structure coherent throughout the sections.

Personal work included extensive research and selection of materials, design of a layout, font selection, keynote presentations. Aside from my magazine section, I conceived the CinePrint section and the Magazine Trailer. The trailer is the result of an accurate editing in Final Cut based on an all-day shooting of the magazine. Plus, we designed an insert that can be unfolded to reveal a poster depicting over 70 Milan underground tickets, each one illustrated by hand with a detail of Noordaâ€™s work or a portrait of the designer in the Milan underground environment.
