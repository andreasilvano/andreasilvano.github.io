Tyto

+ MORE INFO

A large, articulated group project that was born upon almost four months of constant and tireless work. Tyto is an add-on for acoustic wayfinding that can be implemented on every urban navigation app on every platform available.

Personal work included: project concept idea, coordination between group members, presentation video concept, written content creation, tone of voice and text guidelines, global research on Citymapper and iOS devices, audio acquisition and manipulation of Siri voice in iOS 11.


by Alicia Invernizzi, Arianna Dipollina, Jacopo William de Denaro, Andrea Silvano and Alessandro Vergani
