Pretest BVS

Pretest BVS is an annual educational 3-days event dedicated to all the future design students at Politecnico di Milano. It is a study event in preparation to the admission test. We created an immersive identity that could convey the beauty and intriguing aspects of design as well as all the info needed for the event.

The identity included: website, social media banners and imagery, indoor signage, A5 flyers, animated bumper, A5 printed booklet with exercises and design notions, recap video, subscription forms etc.
