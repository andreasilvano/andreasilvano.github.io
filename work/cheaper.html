Project

Medical tourism online is advertised with the language of a product of consumption. We transpose real promos of medical tourism found on Instagram into a digital catalogue. The deals and promos on cosmetic surgeries aim at exposing mass marketing language and objectification.


Role

Group coordination, copywriting, conceptualization, research, voice-acting, video ideation, music supervision


Team

by Martina Andreotti, Elena Buttolo, Marco Gabriele, Alessandro Parca, Federico Pozzi and Andrea Silvano
