Il Giornalino di Gian Burrasca

Project

This project consisted of the ideation and realization of a book trailer based on the Italian children's novel "Il Giornalino di Gian Burrasca" by Vamba. The novel is written in the form of a diary and details the adventures of Giannino, a lively and intense kid. We made a short film to give a taste of the story and encourage people to buy the book. We created an Italian version (original) and an English version (USA).


Role

Concept, screenplay, audio-mixing, voiceover ITA and ENG.


Team

by Alessandro Parca, Marco Gabriele and Andrea Silvano
