This project focus on one of the most famous trap group of the 21th century. Migos, in fact, invented what we now call trap music. This paper concentrates on the sociological impact that the group has had in the past years. Many songs, music video and media languages were analyzed in order to establish patterns in both visual and written language. One of the challenges of this work was to render Migos’ content and style through layout and typography. Another goal of the project was to embody some of Migos’ values into a fictional product. We chose to develop a pot delivery service.

Personal work on this project included: research on typography, section of the magazine, “dealiver” identity, project management, file assembly and printing.


by Riccardo David, Jacopo William de Denaro, Lorenzo Gallo, Emanuele Leonardi, NIcolò Lorenzotti, Alessandro Parca and Andrea Silvano
