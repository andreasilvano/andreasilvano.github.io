Tyto

Project

A large, articulated group project: tyto is an add-on for acoustic wayfinding that can be implemented on every urban navigation app on every platform available. This project was featured in the Fuorisalone DESIGNXDESIGNERS 2019 Exhibition and also on the Poitecnico di Milano website.


Audio Branding - Research ◉     Public Transport - Research ◉     Tyto - Project ◉
Role

Project concept idea, coordination between group members, presentation video concept, written content creation, tone of voice and guidelines, global research on Citymapper and iOS devices, audio acquisition and manipulation of Siri's voice in iOS
