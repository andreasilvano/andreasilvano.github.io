Preview & Pretest

Project

Preview & Pretest is a series of events dedicated to all future design & architecture students at Politecnico di Milano. Preview is an event in which students' projects are shown to "wannabe students" in order to help them understand which academic course is right for them. Pretest is a three-day study event in preparation to the Design admission test & Architecture admission test.

Role

Designing a complex system of brands and application that adpats, evolves and changes over time, while preserving recognizability. An identity that includes websites, Social Media curation, indoor signage, A5 printed booklet with exercises and notions, printed ad material, t-shirts, recap videos, brand guidelines.


Team

Andrea Silvano, Caterina Ghio & Federico Pozzi
